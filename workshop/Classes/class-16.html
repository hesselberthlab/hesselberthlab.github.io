Class 16: Genomic Analysis Vignettes¶§

Class date:2014 Mar 5 Wednesday

Vignettes¶§

We will go over workflows for a few specific genomic analyses. Your final projects should be of similar scope and complexity.

Hypothesis:Cell-type-specific DNase I hypersensitive sites are less evolutionarily conserved than constitutive hypersensitve sites
  1. Determine how similar patterns of DNase I hypersensitivity are across all mapped human cell types.
  2. Calculate mean conservation scores for the matrix of cell types.
  3. Correlate patterns of DHS sensititvity and conservation.
